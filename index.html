ARTICOLLO FOODIE 1 | Enoteca Pinchiorri

    Enoteca Pinchiorri is an Italian restaurant located in the city of Florence.
    Its owners are Giorgio Pinchiorri and Annie Féolde.
    In 2008, the restaurant was named the 32nd best in the world by British Restaurant Magazine.
    The restaurant has been the recipient of the Wine Spectator Grand Prize since 1984.
    It has been consistently awarded three stars by the Michelin Guide since 1993.
    Chef Annie Féolde became the first woman to earn three Michelin stars in Italy.
    Dishes range from a risotto with fregola, pumpkin flowers, saffron and shrimp to lamb topped with dried fruit.
